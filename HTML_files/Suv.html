Something about our SUV's:

BMW offers a range of SUVs, often referred to as Sports Utility Vehicles (SUV's), which combine luxury, performance, and practicality.
Each BMW SUV caters to different preferences, whether you prioritize style, performance, practicality, or luxury.
Here's an overview of our SUV lineup:

The 1 Series.

Model: BMW X1
Petrol engine: Turbocharged inline 4,
Power: 148 Bhp & 329 NM torque,
Transmission: 8 speed automatic,
Front and rear parking sensors
360-degree camera
Lane departure warning

The 3 Series.

Model: BMW X3
Petrol engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4,
Power: 220 Bhp & 380 NM torque,
Acceleration 0-100: 6.2 seconds,
Driving Modes: Eco Pro, Comfort, Sport+,
Digital Instrument Cluster,Climate Control,
Blind-spot monitoring, Heads-up display,

The 5 Series.

Model: BMW X5
Petrol Engine: 3.0-liter inline 6-cylinder,
Power: 340 Bhp & 450 NM torque,
Acceleration 0-100: 5.5 seconds,
8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission,
xDrive (all-wheel drive),
plug in hybrid, lane keep assist.

The Electric luxury SUV.

Model: BMW XM
Engine: 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8,
Electric motor: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission,
Power: 644 Bhp & 590 NM torque,
Acceleration 0-100: 4.1 seconds,
Battery Capacity: 25.7 kWh, M xDrive
ADAS system, iDrive system.
