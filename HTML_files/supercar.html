Something about our Supercars:

BMW is renowned for producing high-performance vehicles that combine luxury, advanced technology,
and thrilling driving dynamics.Here are some highlights of BMW's supercar offerings and concepts:

The M2

Model: BMW M2.

Engine Type: 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder,
8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission,
Power: 473 Bhp & 450 NM torque,
Acceleration 0-100: 4.1 seconds,
14.9-inch BMW Curved Display with iDrive 8,
Dual-zone automatic climate control,
Adaptive M suspension, M Compound brakes,
M Servotronic steering.

The M3

Model: BMW M3

Engine Type: 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder,
Standard M3: 6-speed manual transmission,
M3 CS: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission,
Power: 510 Bhp & 480 NM torque,
Acceleration 0-100: 3.9 seconds,
xDrive all-wheel drive, Three-zone automatic climate control,
Active Driving Assistant, Active Blind Spot Detection,
High-performance summer tyres,

The M5

Model: M5

Engine Type: 4.4L BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8,
Type: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission,
Power: 720 Bhp & 740 NM torque,
Acceleration 0-100: 3.4 seconds,
System: M xDrive all-wheel drive with adjustable 4WD, 4WD Sport, and 2WD modes
Frontal Collision Warning with City Collision Mitigation,
electronically controlled dampers,
M Compound brakes with optional M Carbon ceramic brakes.

The M8

Model: M8

Engine Type: 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8,
Power: 620 Bhp & 560 NM torque,
Type: 8-speed M Sport Automatic Transmission with Drivelogic,
Acceleration 0-100: 3.6 seconds,
M xDrive all-wheel drive with adjustable 4WD, 4WD Sport, and 2WD modes,
