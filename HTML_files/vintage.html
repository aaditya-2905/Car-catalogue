Something about our Vintage cars:

BMW's vintage cars stand as symbols of timeless style and groundbreaking technology,
admired by enthusiasts and collectors alike.
Here's an overview of some of BMW's most iconic vintage models and their impact:

The Vintage 626

"BMW 262" typically refers to the Getrag 262.
BMW E12 5 Series (1972-1981),
inline-six engines,
4-speed manual transmission,
Gear Ratios:-
1st Gear: 3.86:1
2nd Gear: 2.20:1
3rd Gear: 1.40:1

The Vintage 328

BMW 328 is a renowned sports car produced in 1940.
Engine: 1,971 cc M328 straight-6,
Power: 80 Bhp @5000 RPM,
Transmission: 4-speed manual,
Suspension:-
Front: Independent with transverse leaf spring
Rear: Live axle with leaf springs
Hydraulic drum brakes.
